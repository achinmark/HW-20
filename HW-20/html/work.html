Project 2: Federal Communications Commission:
  • The Format: Website & Mobile App
  • The Challenge: The current FCC website was not easily understood due to unconnected pages, broken links, and a chaotic layout.
  • The Research: Our team conducted a Heuristic Evaluation, Redlining, Matrix (Pain Points and Potential Solutions), User Tests (Round 1 - Initial, Round 2 - Interaction, Round 3 - Responsive A/B), Sketching, Cardsorting, Navigation Strategy, and Site Mapping.
  • The Result: The redesigned FCC website and mobile apps helped users spend less time searching and more time acting through clearer formatting and more accessible terminology.
Project 3: Barkee Dog Training:
  • The Format: Mobile App
  • The Challenge: During quarantine, dog owners struggled to find time and resources with which to train their pets with.
  • The Research: Our team conducted a Competitor Analysis (4 apps), Prioritization Matrix, User Flow, Paper to Digital, and User Tests (3 tests, 5 tasks) specifically geared towards dog owners.
  • The Result: With the Barkee App, dog owners can enroll in online training, receive personalized feedback, connect with other owners, and order pet supplies with a couple of simple taps.
